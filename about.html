Meet the Owner

A Passion for Protecting Homes

Bob Pullen started Chem-Tech Fumigation in 1984 in the Fremont, Nebraska area. In 2000, Marty Clausen joined Bob and brought a deeply personal approach to the business. With Bob's retirement, Marty continued to serve customers with the same care and dedication that built the company's reputation.

In 2017, Marty's oldest son Nicholas joined the team, bringing fresh energy and modern expertise. The company also changed its name to Chem-Tech Pest Solutions, reflecting its expanded range of services. Customer satisfaction has always been — and will always be — the #1 goal.

Marty and his wife Tami were born and raised on farms in Nebraska. They've lived in Hooper for over 25 years and have 3 adult children and 9 grandchildren. Marty volunteers on the Hooper Fire Department, serves as an EMT, and is deeply active in his community.

"Our commitment to you and your family is real. Give us a call so we can prove it to you today."

— Marty Clausen, Owner & Founder
