How We Work

Our 4-Step Process

Every residential treatment follows a proven process — no guesswork, no generic spray-and-go.

1

Inspection

We walk the full interior and exterior of your home, identifying active pest activity, harborage areas, and all entry points. Nothing gets missed.

2

Treatment

Targeted application of EPA-approved products — gel baits, residual sprays, and perimeter barrier — focused on where pests actually live, not just where they're visible.

3

Prevention

We identify and address the conditions that attract pests — moisture issues, entry gaps, food sources — and give you specific recommendations to keep them out long-term.

4

Monitoring

Our service plans include follow-up visits to maintain the protective barrier around your home and catch any new activity before it becomes a problem.
