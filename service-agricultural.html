Our Philosophy

Integrated Pest Management

IPM is a science-based approach that prioritizes prevention over reaction — reducing chemical use while achieving better long-term outcomes.

1

Monitor

Regular site assessments track pest populations before they reach economic threshold levels. We catch problems early — not after they've already cost you.

2

Prevent

Sanitation, structural exclusion, harborage reduction, and facility management guidance to eliminate the conditions that support pest populations.

3

Control

When thresholds are exceeded, we apply the most targeted, lowest-impact control method — traps, baits, biologicals — before escalating to chemical treatments.

4

Evaluate

Post-treatment monitoring confirms effectiveness. We document outcomes and adjust the program seasonally to match the real pest pressure on your operation.
